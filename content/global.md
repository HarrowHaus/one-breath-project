# Global microcopy

## Site identity
- **Name:** The One Breath Project
- **Tagline (under the name, everywhere):** Silent & Preventable

## Primary navigation (exactly five)
Learn · Data · Act · Resources · About

## Footer
- **Disclaimer (required, every page):** The One Breath Project is an independent nonprofit. We are not a government agency and not a manufacturer. The information here is for education and does not replace professional inspection or emergency services.
- **Emergency line (always visible in footer):** Suspect carbon monoxide right now? Get outside and call 911. Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222.
- **Footer links:** About · Our sources & methods · Privacy · Accessibility · Contact

## Buttons / calls to action (reuse these labels)
- Primary: "Check my home" · "Send my landlord the request" · "Find help near me" · "See the data"
- Secondary: "Read the full story" · "How we know this" · "Download the checklist"
- Never use guilt or pressure labels. No "No thanks, I don't care" style opt-outs.

## Empty / loading / error states
- **Data not available:** "We don't have verified data for this yet. When we do, it'll appear here with its source." (Do NOT show a placeholder number.)
- **Location off:** "You can turn on location, or type your ZIP code — either works."
- **Form success (request generator):** "Copied. Paste it into an email or text to your landlord."
- **Generic error:** "Something went wrong on our end. Try again in a moment."

## Tone reminders (for any microcopy not listed here)
Plain, calm, declarative. Source in the sentence. One clear action. See `/docs/01_VOICE_GUIDE.md`. If copy isn't here, stop and ask rather than write new user-facing text.
