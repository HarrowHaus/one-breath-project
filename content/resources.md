## Grants & programs (for landlords, housing authorities, and advocates)
Short, accurate blurbs. Verify current program details before relying on them.

- **FEMA Fire Prevention & Safety Grants:** federal grants that can fund fire- and carbon-monoxide-safety projects, including alarms and public education.
- **HUD Healthy Homes:** funding aimed at making housing safer and healthier, which can include carbon monoxide protection.
- **State weatherization assistance (e.g. IHWAP in Illinois):** programs that improve low-income homes and can address combustion-appliance safety.
- **Community Development Block Grants (CDBG):** flexible local funding that communities can direct toward housing safety improvements.

**Note:** program rules and availability change. We link to the official program pages; confirm eligibility there.
