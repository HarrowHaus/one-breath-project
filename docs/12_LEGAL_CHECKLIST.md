# 12 — Legal & 501(c)(3) Checklist (parallel workstream)

These are not build tasks, but the site can't go public without them. Run them alongside the build. None require coding; several need a lawyer.

## Before public launch (required)
- [ ] **Privacy policy** finalized by a lawyer to match your actual tools and jurisdiction (plain-language draft is in `content/about.md`).
- [ ] **Accessibility statement** finalized with a real contact (draft in `content/about.md`).
- [ ] **"Independent nonprofit, not a government site" disclaimer** live in the footer (already specified).
- [ ] **Not impersonating government:** confirm no `.gov` banner or USWDS Identifier is used (see `/docs/02_DO_NOT_BUILD.md`).
- [ ] **Data attribution:** cite CDC, CPSC, USFA, etc. per their public-data terms on the sources/methodology page.
- [ ] **Hero story:** used with attribution as public news reporting (no permission required). Optional courtesy: notify the family; honor any takedown request.

## Organizational (in parallel; not a launch blocker for the site itself)
- [ ] **501(c)(3) formation** underway or complete (incorporation, EIN, IRS application). The site's transparency pages support this.
- [ ] **Board / advisory** members named (fill the placeholders in `content/about.md`).
- [ ] **Funding transparency** statement written once sources are known (placeholder in `content/about.md`).
- [ ] **Manufacturer firewall** documented as policy: editorial independence from any CO-product maker; disclosure of any relationship on any "find protection" step.
- [ ] **Terms of use** drafted (optional but advisable).

## Content integrity gate (every publish)
- [ ] Every figure sourced + Measured/Modeled tagged (`/handoff/FACT_CHECK_LOG.md`).
- [ ] Modeled data labeled modeled; measured data cited with year.
- [ ] No invented statistics, people, or real-time counters.

The site is a credibility vehicle for the nonprofit and its grants. These items are what make it trustworthy to a reviewer, a journalist, and the public.
